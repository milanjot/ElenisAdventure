Eleni the Elephant is late to meet her friend at the other side of the jungle and is stuck between 2 routes

Pick an option!

Eleni sees that many of the other animals are taking the swamp bridge. The only animals that are taking the crosswalk are holding hiking gear. Therefore Eleni will take the swamp bridge.

Eleni’s brochure is showing that the bridge will get her to the other side quicker. But, Eleni sees that there are too many animals on the bridge and it is backed up. Therefore Eleni will not take the swamp bridge.
